# Video Background Setup

To use your autonomous vehicle perception video as the full-page background:

1. **Copy your video** to `frontend/public/`

2. **Supported filenames:**
   - `Create_a_cinematic_AI_visualization_video_of_an_autonomous_vehicle_perception_system.____Scene_descr_seed2434143456.mp4` (exact name)
   - `hero-perception.mp4` (shorter alias — copy and rename if preferred)

3. The app will automatically use the video as a full-page background with a dark overlay for readability. If the video fails to load, a gradient fallback is shown.
